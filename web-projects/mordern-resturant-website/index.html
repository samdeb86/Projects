About La Bella Cucina

Founded by Giovanni and Maria Rossi in 1978, La Bella Cucina began as a small trattoria in the Tuscan countryside. What started as a humble kitchen serving simple, hearty meals to locals has grown into an award-winning dining experience while maintaining its family roots.

Our son, Chef Marco Rossi, now brings his Michelin-star experience back home, blending traditional recipes with modern culinary techniques. Every dish tells a story ‚Äì from the sun-ripened tomatoes in our sauces to the freshly milled flour in our pasta.
